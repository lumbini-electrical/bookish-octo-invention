About Us

Legal Profile & Company Objectives

abiral-infrasolutions Pvt. Ltd. (formerly Abiral Infra Solutions Pvt. Ltd.) is a registered private limited company, established under the Companies Act of Nepal in November 2020 (2077 BS). The company’s registered office is located in Bagmati Province, Kathmandu Metropolitan City, Ward No. 3, Kathmandu District, Nepal.

Our business is legally recognized as both commercial and entrepreneurial, operating in full compliance with relevant Nepali laws and regulations. We are committed to supporting Nepal’s infrastructure growth through innovation and professionalism.

Our Business Scope

  • Planning, development, design, and implementation of infrastructure projects such as hydropower, highways, mechanical and electrical engineering works, buildings, roads, bridges, water supply, irrigation, telecommunications, and more.
  • Supplying and trading machinery, equipment, raw materials, vehicles, generators, agricultural and household goods, electrical and construction materials—sourced locally or imported as permitted by law.
  • Providing engineering consulting, project management, technical supervision, and operational support for sectors such as construction, real estate, energy, and public utilities.
  • Engaging in import/export, distribution, dealership, and supply chain activities related to infrastructure and development materials.
  • Participating in any other lawful business activities that support infrastructure, industrial, environmental, and technological development as permitted by prevailing Nepali law.

abiral-infrasolutions Pvt. Ltd. is dedicated to delivering innovative, reliable, and sustainable infrastructure solutions. Our management and technical team comprises experienced professionals in engineering, consulting, and project management.
