About Us

Who We Are

Lumbini Electrical Proprietary Limited Company is a registered Australian private limited business, operating as LUMBINI ELECTRICAL PTY LTD from Sydney, New South Wales. Founded with a commitment to high-quality service and reliability, our company brings together skilled professionals and a strong dedication to advancing Australia’s electrical and infrastructure sectors.

Our Mission

We aim to deliver innovative and sustainable solutions in electrical engineering, installation, and supply. Our work supports residential, commercial, and industrial clients with a focus on safety, efficiency, and cutting-edge technology.

What We Do

  • Electrical systems design, installation, maintenance, and upgrades for homes, businesses, and industrial facilities.
  • Supply and distribution of electrical components, materials, and equipment—sourced both locally and internationally.
  • Project management and consulting services for new constructions, renovations, and large-scale infrastructure projects.
  • Smart home and automation solutions, renewable energy integration (such as solar), and energy efficiency improvements.
  • Trading, import/export, and supply chain support for electrical goods and related products.
  • Any other legal business activities supporting electrical, technological, or infrastructure advancement in Australia.

Our Values

At Lumbini Electrical, we emphasize quality, safety, transparency, and client satisfaction. Our team combines years of practical expertise with a passion for delivering results that meet or exceed industry standards.
